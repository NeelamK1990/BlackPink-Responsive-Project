Why BlackPink

Why BlackPink is so Popular?

The most significant part of BLACKPINK's popularity lies in the group's undeniable stage presence and their songs and bilingual lyrics, which talks about independence and vulnerability. BLACKPINK is also known for their video styling or "concepts" for each song.They are the first ever K-pop girl group to perform in Coachella.

BLACKPINK has always been known as a girl group with many outstanding international achievements. Their international fanbase is also extremely strong, willing to spend money on idol-related products and contribute significantly to the group’s achievements. Specifically, recently, thanks to the love of fans, especially international fans, BLACKPINK has become a K-Pop girl group to become a million album seller with their first full-album 'The Album'.
